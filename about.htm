WHOLESALE ORGANICS SINCE 1992

We're not ones to blow our own trumpet but if we can't do that on our own website, then where can we do it, right?!

We've been designing and crafting high-quality handmade organic body jewelry since 1992. In other words...we've been doing this for quite some time and so we think it's fair to say that we've been doing more than a few things right. Perhaps just as importantly - we work hard to get better at what we do every single day.

We believe in offering our customers really well-made and interestingly-designed products at competitive prices. It's pretty much as simple as that.

If you're interested in doing business with us or would like to learn more about how we work, then please don't hesitate to get in touch with us, we'd love to hear from you.
