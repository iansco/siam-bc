Double Flare

The Double Flare style has a flare on each side. The Flares are typically around 1.5mm larger than the Wearable Surface. Please Remember - all our products are handmade, so all measurements are approximate.

Single Flare

The Single Flare style has a flare on one side and is flat on the other. The flat side has an o-ring which sits inside a groove to ensure a great fit. The Flare is typically around 1.5mm larger than the Wearable Surface. Please Remember - all our products are handmade, so all measurements are approximate.

Top Hat

The Top Hat style has a slightly larger rounded edge on one side and is flat on the other. The flat side has an o-ring which sits inside a groove to ensure a great fit. The rounded edge is typically around 1.5mm larger than the Wearable Surface. Please Remember - all our products are handmade, so all measurements are approximate.

Flat Flare

The Flat Flare style has a slightly larger rounded edge on both sides. The rounded edges are typically around 1.5mm larger than the Wearable Surface. Please Remember - all our products are handmade, so all measurements are approximate.

Straight with O-Rings

The Straight with O-Rings style is flat on both sides with o-ring's on each side which sit inside grooves to ensure a great fit.

Straight without O-Rings

The Straight without O-Rings style is flat on both sides and has no o-ring's.
