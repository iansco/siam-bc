Help on Size / Gauge

With Plugs and Tunnels, the Size / Gauge is measured across the Wearable Surface, between points A and B in the illustration and not from the flares, which tend to be approximately 1.5mm larger than the Wearable Surface.

With other products, the Size / Gauge is measured across the part of the earring which is inserted into the ear lobe.

Customize It! If our standard sizes don't meet your requirements, please just add a note to your order explaining your requirements and we'll follow-up with you to discuss the details.

Please Remember - all our products are handmade, so all measurements are approximate.
