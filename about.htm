WHOLESALE ORGANICS SINCE 1992

We're not ones to blow our own trumpet but if we can't do that on our own website, then where can we do it, right?!

We've been designing and crafting high-quality handmade organic body jewelry since 1992. In other words...we've been doing this for quite some time and so we think it's fair to say that we've been doing more than a few things right. Perhaps just as importantly - we work hard to get better at what we do every single day.

We believe in offering our customers really well-made and interestingly-designed products at competitive prices. It's pretty much as simple as that.

If you're interested in doing business with us or would like to learn more about how we work, then please don't hesitate to get in touch with us, we'd love to hear from you.

HAND-CRAFTED

Everything we make is made crafted by hand. Sure - some machinery is involved, but we keep that to a minimum. We're not about mass-produced products, each piece of organic body jewelry is carefully, thoughtfully, and conscientiously made by human hands - something we're very proud of.

QUALITY, CONTROLLED.

All our body jewelry may be made by hand but that doesn't mean quality has to suffer. We've been making organic body jewelry for over 20 years and we've never compromised our values when it comes to quality. Every piece is carefully checked to ensure that your own Customers will be genuinely happy, that returns are minimized, and customer satisfaction is...well...guaranteed.

SERVICE & RELIABILITY

We pride ourselves on our ability to offer the highest levels of service to our customers. We work very hard to make sure that our Customers are genuinely satisfied and whenever we do get something wrong, we fix the problem as quickly as we can and learn from our mistakes. We want you to know that you're in good hands when you do business with us.
