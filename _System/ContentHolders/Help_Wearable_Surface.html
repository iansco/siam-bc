Help on Wearable Surface

For Plugs and Tunnels, the Wearable Surface is measured at the part of the earring which comes into contact with your ear lobe, shown as the shaded area (W) in the illustration.

For "Average Ears" the Wearable Surface is 8mm wide but we can also produce earrings in two smaller ("Skinny" and "Extra Skinny") and two larger ("Thicker" and "Big Lobes") Wearable Surface widths.

Customize It! If our standard Wearable Surface sizes don't meet your requirements, please just add a note to your order explaining your requirements - we can usually produce custom Wearable Surface sizes at no extra cost!

Please Remember - all our products are handmade, so all measurements are approximate.
